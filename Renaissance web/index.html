What is Renaissance?

Renaissance is the annual IETE TechFest at KJSIT—a digital showcase of innovation, technology, and creativity. Explore event highlights, animated stories, and the countdown to our next big celebration!
8 Epic Events
"Experience breakthrough innovation through hands-on workshops, dynamic competitions, and inspiring showcases curated for future-ready discovery and collaboration."
CyberX HackFest
Unleash your talent in a cybersecurity coding marathon. Tackle live defense scenarios and build secure apps.
Green Energy Expo
Explore innovations in renewables and sustainability. Meet clean energy pioneers and try interactive demos.
Innovation Odyssey
Discover student inventions and vote for the top innovation at Renaissance’s main showcase.
Drone Tech Arena
Pilot drones in fast-paced races and flight simulations. Learn aerial robotics in interactive workshops.
Medical Robotics Symposium
See healthcare robots in action and debate their impact. Students present next-generation innovations.
BioTech Lab Workshop
Experiment in genetic engineering and molecular diagnostics. Learn with hands-on bioinformatics activities.
Startup Visionaries Pitch
Present your startup to mentors in a live pitch contest. Get feedback and win support for your ideas.
Physics Adventure Park
Solve challenges outdoors with games and live experiments. Teams compete in creative science contests.

What is IETE?

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is a premier professional society advancing futuristic tech, learning, and innovation in electronics, IT, and communications. Bridging visionaries, learners, and industry leaders—IETE is where ideas and talent ignite.
